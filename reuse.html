Andhra Pradesh's SDG Journey

SDG India Index Ranking

Andhra Pradesh has shown significant improvement in SDG implementation, ranking among the top performing states in India. The state achieved a score of 72 on the SDG India Index 2020-21, demonstrating strong progress in sustainable development goals.

Healthcare Achievements

Leading in healthcare indicators with successful implementation of schemes like Dr. YSR Aarogyasri, Jagananna Amma Vodi, and YSR Kanti Velugu. The state has achieved remarkable progress in maternal and child healthcare metrics.

12 SDG Goals On Track in Andhra Pradesh

SDG 1: No Poverty

76%

Implementation of YSR Pension Kanuka and housing schemes has significantly reduced poverty levels. Direct benefit transfers ensure financial support reaches beneficiaries efficiently.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger

82%

Through PDS system modernization and YSR Sampoorna Poshana, malnutrition rates have decreased significantly. Focus on sustainable agriculture practices.

SDG 3: Good Health

85%

YSR Aarogyasri and Kanti Velugu programs have improved healthcare accessibility. Significant reduction in maternal mortality rates.

SDG 4: Quality Education

79%

Nadu-Nedu and Amma Vodi schemes have increased enrollment rates and improved educational infrastructure.

SDG 5: Gender Equality

73%

Women empowerment through YSR Aasara and increased representation in local governance bodies.

SDG 6: Clean Water

77%

Jal Jeevan Mission implementation and improved sanitation facilities across rural areas.

SDG 7: Clean Energy
