SDG 4: Quality Education

Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all

Understanding SDG 4: Quality Education

Quality education is fundamental to creating sustainable development. It is not just about access to education, but ensuring effective learning outcomes, acquiring relevant knowledge and skills for decent work, and developing responsible global citizenship.

Tamil Nadu has consistently been a frontrunner in India's educational development. The state's commitment to education is reflected in its comprehensive policies, innovative programs, and substantial budget allocation to the education sector.
